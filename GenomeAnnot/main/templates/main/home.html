GenomAnnot is a versatile platform designed to accommodate four distinct roles: Reader, Annotator, Validator, and Administrator.

Readers have the capability to explore genomes comprehensively, accessing detailed information about genes and proteins. The platform facilitates the application of Blast using sequences stored in the database. Additionally, users can contribute to the expansion of the database by adding new genomes.

Annotators play a crucial role in enhancing the platform's depth. They can enrich the information associated with genes or proteins, contributing to a more detailed and comprehensive genomic repository.

Validators are tasked with the responsibility of reviewing and approving or disapproving annotations submitted by annotators, ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the information presented on the platform.

Administrators wield the authority to manage task distribution and oversee the progress of the collective efforts. Their role is pivotal in ensuring the smooth functioning of the annotation process and the overall advancement of the platform.
